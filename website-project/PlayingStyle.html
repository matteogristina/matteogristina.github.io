Playing Style

All great players have a unique playing style. This is the same case with Pirlo. He normally plays a central defensive midfielder position. He is a magician on the ball, and can pass to anyplayer on the field, no matter where they are. He is known for frequently making riskier and more difficult passes, in particular for his extremely accurate long-range passing, both on the ground or in the air, with any foot. Also, he is considered to be one of the best passers in the history of the sport, and is also known for his accurate striking ability from distance. His teammates on the Italian National team call him l'architetto (the Architect) because of how he can create plays.

What Pirlo Is Known For

  • Passing
  • Long Passes
  • Shots
  • Free Kicks
