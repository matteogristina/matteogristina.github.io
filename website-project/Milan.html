Milan

Pirlo was sold to rivals A.C. Milan for 33 billon Italian lire (about 20 million U.S. Dollars) on June 30th, 2001. Pirlo led Serie A (Italian Soccer League) in the 2002–03 season in four places – Passes (any) (2589), ball possession (123 hours played and 39 minutes), Completed Long Balls (661), and Completed Passes (2093). He averaged almost 90 passes per game throughout the season. In 2006 to 2009, Pirlo went on to win his second UEFA Champions League title with Milan, also helping his team to finish fourth in Serie A.

Overall With Milan

In total with Milan, Pirlo made 401 appearances, scoring 41 goals.
